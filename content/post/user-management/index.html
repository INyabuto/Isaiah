“If I have seen further, it is by standing on the shoulders of giants.” - Isaac Newton

Managing a bunch of users directly in DHIS2 is not something you would like to do on a late friday afternoon. It gets even worse, when the system is fairly unstable and all what you see is 502 - bad gate way. Its the time to shutdown the laptop and head straight home! In this post, I am going to share a mini guide on how to quickly manage users, maximize on the DHIS2 up time (accomplish much with less time), and keep your friday afternoon in the mood!

Before you start, I recommend reading the following:

  • DHIS2 developers guide
  • PSI DHIS2 Standards
  • DHIS2 and R Integration

Also, to follow along with me: Please set up R and RStudio. See here on how to get started with R programming.
